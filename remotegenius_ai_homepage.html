Frequently Asked Questions

How does the talent verification process work? +
Our three-step process includes identity verification, domain-specific assessments, and a manual review by our talent team. Only candidates who meet all criteria are approved and visible to employers.
Are assessments mandatory for all talent categories? +
Candidates may be required to complete assessments as part of the qualification process. Assessment requirements vary by category and the specific roles you're applying for.
How quickly can I hire talent for my project? +
Most companies make their first hire within 48 hours of posting a job. Our pre-screened talent pool and AI matching ensure you see the most relevant candidates immediately.
What types of work arrangements are supported? +
We support full-time, part-time, contract, freelance, and project-based engagements — all remote. Employers and talent agree on terms through our secure platform.

All data is encrypted in transit and at rest. We follow industry-standard security practices and are committed to user privacy and data protection.
